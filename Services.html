We serve all varieties of chocolate

But... What is the difference between white, dark and milk chocolate?

  • White Chocolate, to start with, is made of cocoa butter, a pale yellow vegetable fat which has a cocoa aroma and flavor, adding sugar and vanilla to it. It contains no cocoa solids, so practically its not a chocolate, but contains more fat and sugar.
  • Milk chocolate is made of cocoa solids, but with added ingredients like, milk powder, milk liquid or condensed milk. There certain requirments for chocolate, at least 25% should be cocao solids, at least 12% milk soilds and 10% chocolate liquor. Sugar and other aromas are being added to intensify flavor.
  • Dark Chocolate does not contain any milk or other milk solids. So, we can say that Dark Chocolate is the real deal. The percentage of cocoa solids is much higher, 30% to 80%. It is more robust in taste, because it has the real chocolate taste of bitterness. Important to mention is, that It is an excellent source of fiber and minerals.
