Facts about chocolate

  3. Dark chocolate contains the most antioxidants that helps your heart and reduces blood pressure.
  4. Eat dark chocolate every day to minimize heart disease
  5. Chocolate substitutes coffee, gives you energy
  6. Helps with depression, Tumors and high blood pressure.
